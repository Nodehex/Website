ABOUT NODEHEX

Nodehex is an information management specialist group for hyperscale data centers and commercial builds. Our team has a wealth of experience delivering and managing documentation services across a vast number of hyperscale datacenter projects and the specialist infrastructure, offices and ancillary buildings that enable them. We simplify the process by removing the ambiguity around documentation, understanding client needs and requirements and then implementing a structure to ensure a lasting impression for information management across all mission-critical systems.

Our services focus on providing a high quality, data driven and front-loaded effort to ensure an effective delivery from design to decommissioning.
